Features and About

The Task

This was a game made as part of an assignment for the Programming for 3D module.

This project was worked on alone, wth only assets being borrowed from the Unity asset store of the player model, the detective model and the buildings surrounding.

Features

When working on this project, the goal was to create a feeling of suspense and fear of the unknown.

All lighting, the player's movement, particle effects, other textures, displaying of text with UI, animations of doors, physics of objects, and in particular the fire and satanic symbol were all coded by myself. Audio was selected for the game, and scripts were made to augment it most effectively.

In particular, using the HD Render Pipeline had it's challenges, as this was my first exposure to it. This enabled me to learn how to use the shader graphs to create the satanic fire in the centre of the map.

Inspirations

The most pressing inspiration for this game was the game "SCP - Containment Breach", but a second and also important inspiration was "Phasmophobia".

The level design took inspiration from Phasmophobia, hoping to make the player feel trapped into certain rooms, while some "demonic entity" is moving around.

The inspiration from SCP was there to make the player feel at unease, and make them unsure of what is happening while trying to maneuveur in the dark.
