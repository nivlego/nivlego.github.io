Introduction and
About me

I am a highly motivated and driven individual with a deep passion for game development, constantly seeking opportunities to learn and improve myself. I hold a 1st Class degree in Computer Science Games and Multimedia Environments from the University of Sussex as of 2023.

My journey into the world of game development began during my secondary school days when my fascination with video games led me to build my own PC and delve into the specifics of computer hardware. This passion, coupled with an innate curiosity for programming, led me to pursue Computer Science in my academic studies. Today, I am actively seeking a career in the game development industry, particularly as a Gameplay Programmer, where I can apply my skills and contribute meaningfully to creating immersive gaming experiences.

Proficient in C# and well-versed in the Unity Engine, as well as having experience in Java and Python, I have already achieved a solid foundation in game development technologies. Additionally, I have been actively broadening my skillset by continuing previous exploration of Javascript, C, and C++ while also working towards learning the Unreal Engine. With a keen eye for detail, a thirst for knowledge, and a determination to excel in the field, I am excited to embark on a journey of continuous learning and professional growth within the game development industry. I am eager to contribute my expertise and creativity to the development of innovative and captivating games.
