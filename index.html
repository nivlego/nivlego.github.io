My Skills

Programming Languages

I have knowledge with C#, and moderate knowledge with Java. I have also used C, C++ and Python in the past and I'm currently working on improving my C++ skills. In addition, I have knowledge of HTML and CSS, with minor Javascript ability.

Game Engines

I have used the Unity game engine for 2+ years at the University of Sussex, and I am currently learning the Unreal Engine along with C++ skills.

Software

I have experience with both modelling and animation softwares: Blender and Cinema4d. In addition, I have experince using the Adobe suite including Photoshop, Premiere Pro, After Effects and Adobe XD. I have also spent time in the past using Git, in addition to Fork, the Git management software.

Integrated Development Environments

I have used many Development Environments throughout my time at university and before. These include IDLE for Python 3, Pycharm, Dev-C++, Jetbrains IntelliJ, Jetbrains Rider, Visual Studio and VSCode, BlueJ & Eclipse.

Soft Skills

Teamwork

- Worked in multiple group projects during University each scoring 1st class grades, with a leading role in one and supporting leading role in another.

- Participated in a team for two different Global Game Jams (2022, 2023), and working in a short space of time.

Communication

- Regularly participated in TTRPG group of 4+ people, where improvisation and presenting as a character were central for the last 4 years.

- Scored First classes in 4 presentations during my time at University, where I was a central speaker, or the only speaker.

Leadership

- Led a group of video production students to a first class over a semester, and dealt/managed many interpersonal problems that arose in addition to several other leadership roles at University.

- Organise, Plan and Run regular TTRPG group sessions of around 4+ hours each week.
